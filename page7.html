The orchard rustled with wind and whispers. Apples glowed faintly with morning dew.

A gnarled tree bore a symbol etched deep into the bark—a stag with a compass.

Thistlewick tapped it. “This was their first mark. Feel it?”

The child placed a hand on the bark. A low hum rose beneath their fingers.

“Memory is stored in wood,” Thistlewick said. “The Order hid something here...”
