Thistlewick hovered just above the sundial, wings fluttering like dandelion fluff.

“Come closer,” he whispered, “this park was once guarded by the Order of the Deerheart. They left clues hidden in plain sight—but only for those who believe.”

He blinked, and a faint shimmer revealed an old stone path leading toward the orchard.

“Your journey begins there.”
