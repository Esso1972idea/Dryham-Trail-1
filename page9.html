Suddenly, a rustle in the hedge. A creature emerged:

A fox—but not quite. Its fur shimmered with leaves, eyes glowing amber.

“This is Marnix, a forest warden,” Thistlewick said. “He guards the second clue.”

Marnix bowed its head and padded deeper into the trees.

“Follow him, but stay quiet. The woods are listening.”
