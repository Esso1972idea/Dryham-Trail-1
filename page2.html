A soft breeze stirred the ancient yew trees. Thistlewick leapt into the air and hovered beside the child’s ear.

“Long ago, this land hid a powerful treasure—the Deerheart Compass. It could guide the bearer through any maze, solve any riddle.”

He fluttered forward.

“But it was broken and scattered across Dyrham. Lost… until now.”
