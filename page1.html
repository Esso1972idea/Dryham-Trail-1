Thistlewick blinked into existence atop the mossy sundial.

“Ah, you’re here!” he squeaked. “Dyrham Park has been whispering secrets again.”

The sundial trembled as sparkles danced around his wings. He looked up at the child with wide, expectant eyes.

“There’s a mystery in the air. One only a brave explorer like you can solve.”
